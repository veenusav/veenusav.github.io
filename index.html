/ engagement Models

We do programing for behind the screen technologies of interactive computer graphics. More than coding, this include technology selection, architecting and after deployment support based on the queries/issues based on platform considerations.

We only work on outcome based contracts rather than time & material contracts. We start our engagement in two ways with a potential customer.

1.      Proposal

2.      Technical analysis report & estimate

Depending upon the clarity of the solution and complexities of target hardware and input data, a proposal phase can have a ball park estimate both on time and price. Normally proposals are made free of cost. As our expertise is normally engaged, scheduling for a proposal takes time. So this unpaid method is apt for those who have time and R&D mindset.

Technical Analysis report with estimation are paid engagements though its output is not fully charged. Please note that the functional specification stage which follows this stage will give complete clarity on the features of the solution. Analysis report will only give the solution is feasible or not. We have a history of 86% success rate on estimate and technology selection. The rest had requirement revamping during the course of the time. Depending on the complexity the price ranges from 3000 USD to 30,000 USD. Normally this work will reduce effort in reduction of work-product estimate as we are getting clarity and time. So we suggest to industrialists to start with this engagement model.
